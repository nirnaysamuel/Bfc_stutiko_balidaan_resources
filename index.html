Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 1, 2026

Bethlehem Fellowship Songs App ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

1. No Personal Data Collection

We do not require users to create an account, register, or provide any personal information (such as name, email address, or phone number) to use the App. The App is designed to be used without any user identification.

2. No Analytics or Tracking

We do not use third-party analytics tools (such as Google Analytics or Firebase Analytics) to track your behavior, song preferences, or usage patterns within the App.

3. Permissions and Internet Access

The App only requires Internet Access to perform the following functions:

  • Retrieving song metadata and YouTube links from our database.
  • Loading external YouTube content (when you click a song link).
  • Fetching current App configurations (such as payment details or feature toggles).

4. Payments and Donations

The "Bless Us" or donation feature in the App is for informational purposes only.

  • No Transactions: The App does not process payments, handle credit card data, or interface directly with banking apps.
  • Information Only: The App displays a static UPI ID and a QR code. Any transaction you choose to make is performed entirely within your own third-party UPI application (e.g., GPay, PhonePe, Paytm).
  • No Data Storage: We do not see, collect, or store any of your financial or banking information.

5. Third-Party Services

Our App contains links to YouTube. When you click these links, you are subject to YouTube’s Privacy Policy and Terms of Service. We are not responsible for the data practices of external platforms.

6. Children’s Privacy

Since we do not collect any personal information, our App is safe for users of all ages, including children. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
